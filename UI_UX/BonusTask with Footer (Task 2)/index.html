Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) is a student-led community supported by Google Developers. We help students bridge the gap between theory and practice by building real-world solutions using modern technologies.

At GDSC NIT Calicut, we foster innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning through workshops, hackathons, and impactful projects.
